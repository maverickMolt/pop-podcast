About the Show

Science myth-busting.
On the record.

The Power of the Point applies a rigorous three-verdict framework to the health and science claims flooding your feed. Upheld. Debunked. Or — most importantly — Misframed.

Because most bad health science isn't fabricated. It's real data, stripped of context, scaled to a headline. We name that mechanism. We explain it. And we give you the tools to spot it yourself.

No wellness theater. No false balance. Just the study, the claim, and our verdict.

✓ UPHELD

The science holds up. We tell you why, and what the evidence actually shows.

✗ DEBUNKED

The claim failed review. We show our work.

~ MISFRAMED

The most important verdict. Real data. Wrong conclusion. We expose the gap.
